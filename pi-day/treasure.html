Treasure Hunt — Rules

  1. This is a team event. Stay with your team at all times.
  2. Each team will receive a starting clue. Solve it to find the location of the next clue.
  3. Clues are hidden across the campus — stay within designated boundaries.
  4. Do not disturb, move, or damage any clue after finding it — other teams need them too.
  5. No running inside buildings. Be respectful of ongoing classes and activities.
  6. No phones or internet to search for answers — rely on your team's brainpower.
  7. The first team to find the final treasure and return to the starting point wins.
  8. In case of disputes, the organiser's decision is final.
