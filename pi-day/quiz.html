Quiz — Rules

  1. This is a team event. Each team will be given answer sheets to write down their answers.
  2. The quizmaster will display the questions on the projector.
  3. Once the timer starts, your team has 1–5 minutes to solve the questions depending on the difficulty.
  4. When the timer runs out, the question is locked — no more time will be given.
  5. Write your final answers clearly on the answer sheet provided.
  6. No phones, calculators, or internet allowed during the round.
  7. Any form of misconduct, will lead to immediate disqualification.
  8. The quizmaster's decision is final in all matters.
