Pi Digit Memorization — Rules

  1. This is an individual event. Each participant competes on their own.
  2. You must recite the digits of Pi (3.14159...) from memory, in order.
  3. No notes, phones, or aids of any kind are allowed.
  4. You will recite the digits aloud to a judge who will verify them in real time.
  5. On your first wrong digit, your attempt ends — the last correct digit is your final count.
  6. You may take brief pauses to recall, but excessively long pauses (over 10 seconds) will end your turn.
  7. The participant who correctly recites the most digits wins.
  8. In case of a tie, a speed round will be used as a tiebreaker.
